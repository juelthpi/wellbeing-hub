ABOUT US

Wellbeing is a healthcare service provider dedicated to ensuring quality medical care and health services for Bangladeshi expatriates and their families worldwide. We understand the healthcare challenges faced by expatriates and offer free telemedicine, health insurance, mental health support, and emergency medical assistance. We also ensure healthcare services for their families in their absence. Our goal is to provide affordable, accessible, safe, and reliable healthcare so that Bangladeshi expatriates can ensure their and their families' health security even while staying abroad.

More

OUR MISSION

  • Ensure Accessible, Affordable, and High-Quality healthcare for Bangladeshi expatriates and their families.

  • Provide Digital Healthcare Solutions through telemedicine, mobile applications, and AI- driven health assessments.

  • Collaborate with Internationally Recognized Healthcare Providers to facilitate global medical support.

  • Establish a Robust Emergency Medical Response System for expatriates in case of health crises.

  • Promote Preventive Healthcare awareness, ensuring regular health check-ups and wellness programs.

  • Support expatriates’ families in Bangladesh with Exclusive Medical Facilities.

  • Offer Multilingual Medical Support, ensuring seamless communication between doctors and patients.

  • Build a Secure and Efficient Healthcare Ecosystem through partnerships with hospitals, clinics, and insurers.
